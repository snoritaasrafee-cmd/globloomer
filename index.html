About Bloom Monitoring

BloomWatch tracks plant growth using NDVI (Normalized Difference Vegetation Index) derived from NASA MODIS satellite data. NDVI shows plant health and helps predict flower bloom events.

Data Sources:

  • MODIS Vegetation Indices
  • Landsat 8/9
  • NASA POWER API
  • GLOBE Observer
